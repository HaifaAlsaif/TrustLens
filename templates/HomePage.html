About Us

The Problem

The surge of AI-generated content makes it increasingly difficult to distinguish between human and machine-created text, especially in short conversations and news articles. This threatens trust and authenticity in digital communications.

Our Solution

TrustLens provides multi-format evaluation with interactive explainability, showing not just the classification but why a decision was made. Built on crowdsourced datasets for real-world accuracy.

Features

Comprehensive tools for detecting and understanding AI-generated content

Multi-Format Evaluation

Analyze both conversations and news articles with specialized detection algorithms for each format.

Interactive Explainability

Understand why content is classified as human or AI-generated with detailed, transparent explanations.

Crowdsourced Dataset

Built on real-world data validated by expert examiners across multiple domains and specializations.

Authenticity Arena

Collaborative platform where examiners contribute to improving detection accuracy through feedback.
